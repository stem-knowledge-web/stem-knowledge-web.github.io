Hello there!

STEM Knowledge is a project that aims to provide well researched, SEO-free, and crisp pedagogical content for learning science subjects.

Started in 2023, this project aims to be like Wikipedia – open, community driven, exhaustive, and free from SEO (that is aimed only at generating clicks), but a learning resource rather than a reference.

We aim to develop an **up-to-date knowledge base** for pure and applied sciences that can teach anyone anything. Our aim is to be an online “super-book” for scientific subjects, by having examples, problems, solutions, etc. but without the constraints of a physical book.

We seek contributions from professors, researchers, domain experts etc. who wish to share their expertise and knowledge with everyone. Please visit our [Github](https://github.com/stem-knowledge-web) repository to make [contributions](/contributing).

This resource is and will always be free of promotions and advertisements.

Happy learning! 🙂

## Features

- Free and up-to-date content with latest validated research
- No back-referencing topics / diagrams / equations. Repeat the content / graphics as much as you want
- free and open-source
- SEO-free. No extra fluff needed
