## Hello there fellow learner!

STEM Knowledge is an ***free and open-source*** (FOSS) knowledge repository for all things in the field of
- science
- technology
- engineering
- mathematics

We aim to build an *SEO-free* pedagogical source where knowledge is crisp and absolute, refined to its simplest form for the learners.

Inspired from [cp-algorithms](https://cp-algorithms.com/index.html){:target="_blank"}, we aim to grow as a knowledge aggregation platform, up-to-date with the latest research and advancements in the corresponding fields.
